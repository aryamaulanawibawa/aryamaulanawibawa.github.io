Crazy rice

Ingredients:

1.Rice
2.Salt and Sugar
3.Onion pree(optional)
4.Onion and garlic
5.eggs
6.sausage
7.Meatballs
8.White Pepper
9.oyster sauce
10.sesame oil
11.Fish sauce
12.ketchup
13.Soy sauce
14.Salty Soy sauce

Step:

1.Pour a little oil in the pan
2.pour a sausage and meatballs to the pan
3.pour a meshed onion and garlic
4.pour a egg inthe pan and stir it
5.pour all the sauce and fry it until all ingredients mixed
6.pour the salt,sugar and white pepper into the pan
7.after all ingredients mixed pour onion pree
8.pour all ingredients to rice and now crazy rice is ready to eat
